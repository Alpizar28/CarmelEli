Clinical Social Worker with over 10 years of therapeutic experience

I approach therapy as a living, ongoing practice. My work is centered on supporting people in developing awareness, emotional responsibility, and a deeper relationship with themselves and others.

Through presence, reflection, and conscious choice, meaningful change becomes possible — not by force, but through steady inner movement.

I believe that growth happens when we slow down enough to listen inward. In therapy, I accompany you in exploring patterns, building emotional clarity, and creating differentiation — allowing change to emerge naturally, at its own rhythm.

"I bring the same therapeutic values into my personal life — practicing conscious choice, emotional presence, and balance within family and relationships."

Research-based therapeutic approaches for personal development

The therapeutic work in this practice is solution-focused and change-oriented, grounded in emotional awareness, personal responsibility, and conscious inner movement.

Differentiation Therapy

Supporting the ability to stay connected while remaining emotionally autonomous. Developing clarity between thoughts and emotions, and strengthening responsibility for one's inner world and choices.

Mindfulness

Cultivating presence in the body and mind. Learning to respond consciously rather than react automatically, creating space for choice and emotional regulation.

Emotion-Focused Therapy

Exploring emotional patterns within relationships. Creating safer, more honest connection with oneself and others through emotional understanding and attunement.
