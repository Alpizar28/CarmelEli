About Carmel

I live and breathe my profession. My central focus is helping you achieve your dream - through improving relationships, taking responsibility, and creating new patterns, healing and growth become possible.

I believe everyone can create meaningful change in their life. I accompany you on the path to realizing your dream, while building practical tools for a fuller, more satisfying life.

"I apply my therapeutic approach in my personal life - happily married and raising my children with conscious choice and balance."
